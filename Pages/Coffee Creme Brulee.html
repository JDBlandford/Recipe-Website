Coffee Creme Brulee

Ingredients Needed

  • Instant Coffee
  • Egg Yolks
  • Heavy or double cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan heat your cream until just beginning to steam but not boiling
  2. Meanwhile whisk your egg yolks with the sugar until fully combined
  3. Slowly whisk your hot cream into the egg mixture to avoid cooking the eggs
  4. add the mixture back to the sauce pan with the vanilla extract and instant coffee
  5. lightly simmer until the coffee smell is strong
  6. pour into your ramekins placed in a water bath
  7. bake until the custard is set
  8. melt your sugar on top, either the traditional way with a torch or by melting in in a pan and pouring over top

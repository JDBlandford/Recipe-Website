Braised Shortribs

Ingredients Needed

  • Bone-in shortribs
  • Carrots, onions, celery, and garlic
  • beef stock and optionally red wine
  • herbs of your choice (rosemary, thyme, sage etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a wide bottom, oven safe pan, sear your shortribs until brown all the way around. remove and set aside
  2. Saute the carrots until slightly soften before adding your other vegetables. Cook until fragrant
  3. deglace with your wine (if you don't want to use wine, replace with beef stock and skip the next step)
  4. Simmer your wine until there is no alcohol smell remaining
  5. Re-add your beef before covering with stock and chopped herbs
  6. in the oven, cook at 250f until fork tender and falling off the bone
  7. remove your beef and vegetables from the braising liquid and simmer until thickened into a rich glaze

Bacon Chili Jam

Ingredients Needed

  • Uncooked bacon
  • Seeded mild chilis
  • Sweetener of choice (Sugar, brown sugar, molases, maple syrup, etc.)/li>
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Yellow Onion
  • Coffee

Instructions

  1. Cook your bacon until crispy and remove from pan
  2. in the rendered bacon fat, saute your onions and garlic until fragrant and soft before adding your peppers
  3. deglaze with coffee and vinegar before adding your sweetener of choice
  4. add the bacon back and simmer on low heat until the liquid is almost fully evaporated and the jam is at the right texture

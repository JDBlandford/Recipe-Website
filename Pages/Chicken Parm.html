Chicken Parm

Ingredients needed

  • Chicken Breast
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Italian Breadcrumbs
  • Dried Parsley, Oregano, and other herbs of your choice (optional)
  • Parmesan and Mozzarella Cheese
  • Olive Oil and Butter
  • Marinara Sauce

Instructions

  1. Butterfly and pound out your chicken
  2. Prepare your breading. in three bowls, combine your flour and seasons, beat your eggs and mix your breadcrumbs with half of your parmesan cheese
  3. cover your chicken in each, making sure it is fully covered and all excess is shaken off
  4. Heat up your oil and butter in a wide bottom, high rim frying pan
  5. Lay your breaded cutlets down into the oil, and fry, flipping once, until golden brown on both sides before draining on a wire rack or paper towels
  6. cover your cutlets in sauce and layer your remaining cheeses on top
  7. preheat your oven to 425f or turn on the broiler setting. Place chicken on a sheet and page until the cheese is fully melted and beginning to bubble
